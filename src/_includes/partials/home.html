Armchair Traveller

Before we lived in other countries, we devoured travel books by the great and the not so great. This is where we will review classic and current travel literature.

Expat Living

Expat living. Not the same as going on holiday! We will have articles, blog posts and nuggets of wisdom from people who have lived as expats or are permanent expats.

Slow Travel

Slow travel is defined in many ways. It can mean making less impact on the planet, or the way that we see it, spending at least long enough to find out a bit about living in a particular place. We want to hear the stories and experiences.
